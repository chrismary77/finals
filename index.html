One Piece Film: Red

One Piece: Film Red follows the Straw Hat Pirates as they embark on a thrilling new adventure. They encounter the world-famous diva Uta and unravel the mysteries of her past, while facing exciting challenges that test their courage and friendship.

This action-packed movie blends music, comedy, and epic battles, highlighting the bonds of the Straw Hat crew as they navigate through danger and adventure.

Release Date: August 6, 2022

Movie Highlights

  • Introduction of Uta, the world-famous diva
  • Epic battles involving the Straw Hat Pirates
  • Musical-themed story arc
  • Exciting adventures across the Grand Line

Official Movie Trailer

Synopsis

The Straw Hat Pirates meet Uta, a famous singer and daughter of Red-Haired Shanks. They uncover secrets about her past and face conflicts that challenge their bonds as a crew, all while music and adventure collide in a spectacular story.
